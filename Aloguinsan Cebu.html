    FARMHOUSE
    You can drink organic juice and beverages and explore different vegetables and fruits

    Eperience a slice of authentic Cebuano culture in Bojo, a river village two kilometers from the town center. Local fishermen and their families are protecting the Bojo River Nature Reserve which includes the 1.4 kilometers-long Bojo river and its riparian zone, home to about 61 bird species and remarkable native flora.
